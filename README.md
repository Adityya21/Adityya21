## 👋 About Me

I'm a pre-final year B.Tech Computer Engineering student at **NMIMS Shirpur (Batch 2027)**, specialising in applied machine learning, statistical modelling, and production AI systems. I work across the full ML lifecycle — from hypothesis-driven EDA and feature engineering to model deployment and business impact measurement.

My focus: building systems that are **explainable, reproducible, and measurable**. I don't just train models — I ship them, interpret them, and tie them to real decisions.

> Currently exploring **LLM-integrated pipelines**, **multi-agent AI systems**, and **deep learning for time-series forecasting**.
